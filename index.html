Six verdicts, zero ambiguity

Every claim receives one of six transparent ratings. No vague "it's complicated." Each verdict comes with cited evidence you can check independently.

TRUE
Accurate and supported by reliable, verifiable evidence.
MOSTLY TRUE
Largely accurate with minor omissions or imprecision in details.
MIXED
Contains both verifiable and unverifiable or inaccurate elements.
MOSTLY FALSE
Significant inaccuracies. May contain a kernel of truth but misleads overall.
FALSE
Not supported by evidence. Directly contradicts known, verified facts.
UNVERIFIABLE
Insufficient public evidence exists to confirm or refute the claim.
