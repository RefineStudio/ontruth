Six-level verdict system

Six verdicts, zero ambiguity

Most fact-checkers give you a binary. Reality is more nuanced. OurTruth uses six calibrated verdicts — each with transparent sourced reasoning — to reflect the true epistemic state of a claim.

TRUE
"The Eiffel Tower grows taller in summer."
Thermal expansion is documented: the tower height increases ~15cm during hot weather. Confirmed by structural engineering data.
MOSTLY TRUE
"Exercise outperforms medication for anxiety."
Multiple RCTs support this for mild-moderate cases. Severe anxiety disorders typically still require pharmacological intervention.
MIXED
"Social media causes teen depression."
Some studies find correlation; others find no causal link. Evidence is actively contested among researchers — no consensus yet.
MOSTLY FALSE
"Humans only use 10% of their brains."
Neuroimaging shows near-total brain activity. The 10% figure is a persistent myth with no scientific origin or support.
FALSE
"The Great Wall is visible from space."
Too narrow (~5-8m) to see from low Earth orbit with the naked eye. Multiple astronauts confirm this.
UNVERIFIABLE
"Governments are hiding UFO evidence."
No publicly verifiable evidence is available. Classified materials cannot be independently assessed.
